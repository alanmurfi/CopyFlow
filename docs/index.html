Built for people who copy things all day and need them organized.

🔍

Instant search

Find any clip from your history in milliseconds. Search by content, and results update as you type.

📌

Pin important clips

Keep your most-used snippets pinned to the top. Email addresses, code fragments, anything you paste often.

✏️

Edit saved clips

Fix typos or tweak saved clips without re-copying. Edit inline and save with Ctrl+Enter.

🖱️

Right-click to paste

Right-click any text field and pick a clip from your history. No need to open the popup every time.

✅

Visual copy confirmation

See a toast notification on screen every time you copy something. You'll always know it was saved.

💾

Export and import

Download your entire clipboard history as JSON. Import it on another machine. Your data is never locked in.

🔒

Optional AES-256 encryption

Enable password-based encryption to protect sensitive clips at rest. Uses AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation — the same standard used by password managers.

⏱️

Auto-lock

When encryption is enabled, CopyFlow locks automatically after a configurable period of inactivity. Your clips stay private even if you walk away.

⚡

Text snippets

Save shortcuts that expand to full text. Type a trigger like /addr and it instantly pastes your home address. No more retyping the same things.
