## Short Description (max 132 chars)

## Detailed Description (max 16,000 chars)

Tired of losing things you copied 5 minutes ago? CopyFlow saves every piece of text you copy and keeps it searchable, organized, and always available.

WHAT COPYFLOW DOES
- Automatically saves everything you copy
- Search your entire clipboard history instantly
- Pin important clips so they stay at the top
- Edit saved clips directly in the popup
- Right-click any text field to paste from your history
- Visual copy confirmation — see a toast on screen every time you copy
- Export and import your clips as JSON backups
- Dark mode and light mode
- Keyboard shortcut (Alt+Shift+V) to open instantly

WHY COPYFLOW?
Most clipboard managers ask for permissions they don't need — like your browsing history, or access to all your data on every website. CopyFlow only requests the minimum permissions required to read your clipboard and store your clips locally. That's it. No analytics. No tracking. No accounts.

Your data stays on your machine. Always exportable. Never locked in.

PRIVACY FIRST
- No browsing history access
- No data sent to any server
- No account required
- All clips stored locally in your browser
- Export your data anytime as a JSON file

FEATURES
Search: Find any clip instantly with real-time search
Pin: Keep your most-used clips at the top
Edit: Fix typos or update saved clips without re-copying
Right-click paste: Paste clips directly into any text field from the context menu
Copy toast: See a visual confirmation every time you copy something
Auto-cleanup: Old unpinned clips are automatically removed after 30 days
Export/Import: Back up your clips and restore them on any machine
Dark mode: Easy on the eyes for late-night work
Keyboard shortcut: Alt+Shift+V opens CopyFlow instantly

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
- clipboardRead/Write: Read and write to your clipboard (the core feature)
- storage: Save your clip history locally
- offscreen: Required by Chrome to access the clipboard in the background
- activeTab/tabs: Record which page you copied from (shown as source info)
- contextMenus: Right-click paste menu
- scripting: Insert pasted text into form fields

CopyFlow is free to use. Built by a solo developer who was tired of losing copied text.
